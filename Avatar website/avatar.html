THE MOVIE


Summary

In the alien world of Pandora live the Na'vi People, beings who appear sensitive but are highly evolved (and large). Because Pandora's environment is poisonous, human/Na'vi hybrids, called Avatars, must link to human minds to allow for free movement on Pandora. Jake Sully, a paralyzed former Marine, becomes mobile again through one such Avatar after being found by the military because he looked like his brother. Sully falls in love with a Na'vi woman named Neytiri. As a bond with her grows, he is drawn into a battle for the survival of her world, and a fight against human people to protect the Na'vi tribes.

The Experiment

The humans were trying to locate gold and oil that was located under their "Home Tree". These Avatars were created for the scientist and people who have studied their culture, to gain their trust and educate them on why the humans need to get the resources from Pandora.

Avatar Sequels Release Date

There are currently 4 announced sequels of the movie that was released almost 10 years ago.

  • Avatar 3 arrives on December 22, 2023.

  • Avatar 5 will premiere on December 17, 2027.
